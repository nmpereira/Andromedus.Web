About Us

A team of talented consultants with digitization experience across North America. Providing localized services in ERP Implementation and Digital transformation for medium to large size organizations.


ERP IMPLMENTATION

Our team takes a deep dive into each business operations of your company. Analysing each individual function and process of your business to effectively suggest an ERP to match your specific needs, whether it be manufacturing specific like Epicor or something to assist in financial tracking like QuickBooks; our team will help you tactfully implement it every step of the way.


DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION

We guide management and help deliver state of the art decision-making resources through careful analysis of the current business and their net impact. We look for opportunities for process automation through top-of-the-line SaaS software. We then tactically help implement these software automations ideas and initiatives, to improve the overall business outlook, generating overall growth to attain a competitive advantage.


PROCESS OPTIMIZATION

If you find your business is losing precious time and resources on processes too difficult or complicated to handle? Our technical consultants are trained to develop custom solutions in order to address difficult problems. We offer a full-service analysis and solution implementation of multiple new processes for your business, making your business run smoother than ever. Some solutions may involve custom app development which your staff can interact with.


FUND RAISING

We guide you through your buy or sell side pitch. Assist and help broker your start-up mission through a variety of mediums fit for your specific needs. Whether it be by putting you in contact with the top VC firms, incubators, or angel investors in North America. Thereby, increasing the chances of a higher start-up valuation relative to other firms, and assist in building connections with the top accelerators and incubators in North America, to guide your mission and grow your valuations.
